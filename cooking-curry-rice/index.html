Cooking - Curry Rice

March 15, 2020

佛蒙特咖哩 (Apple)

Preparation (2 meals)

  • oil
  • rice
  • curry blocksx2
  • onionsx1 (slice or dice)
  • potaoex1
  • carrotx1
  • chicken breast

Process

  1. Pour a bit oil into pot
  2. Add onions
  3. Fry it until it gets soft
  4. Add chicken
  5. Fry it until its colour gets changed
  6. Add carrots
  7. Add water, water should be at the same level as ingredients
  8. Boil the water
  9. Add potatoes and turn low-heat, and put on the lid
  10. Boil it for 10 minutes
  11. 撈浮沫
  12. Turn off the stove
  13. Add curry blocks and stir them unitl melted
  14. Turn on low heat and boil them for 10 mins
  15. Adjust the stickness that you desire, if you want it sticker, cook longer.
