Spaghetti

brand: (recommendation: La Molisana)

  1. boil water
  2. add spaghetti and salt into boiling water
  3. pour some oil
  4. cook it for 8 mins (estimated time on package minus 1~2 mins, about 8~10 mins before fully cooked)
  5. take spaghetti out, and pour some oil (to prevent it form sticking to each other)
  6. get some water out of pot with a bowl for later use)

Classic Tomato Spaghetti

Preparation

  • salt
  • olive oil
  • spaghetti
  • basil
  • garlics
  • onions
  • beef minces (optional)
  • tomato sauce

Process

Cook noodle first, don’t add too much salt, because cheese and guanciale is satly

  1. Turn on low heat
  2. Add onions, stir it until onions turn its colour into transparent
  3. Add garlic and basil and mushroom (optional)
  4. Stir
  5. (optional) Add beef mince and stir it until it is fully cooked
  6. Add tomato sauce, some water, salt (a bit) and black pepper (more), and leave it until it is boiling
  7. Add spaghetti
  8. Add a lot of water
  9. Put them on the plate
  10. Add basil
  11. Add parmesan

Carbonara

Preparation

  • 1 egg + Parmesan cheese and beat the egg
  • guanciale or pancetta or bacon with lots of fat

Process

Cook noodle first, don’t add too much salt, because cheese and guanciale is satly

  1. Pour the oil
  2. Add guanciale to the pan
  3. Add black pepper until guanciale gets darker
  4. Turn the heat off
  5. Add pasta into the pan and add some water (use the water to stop the frying, should be quite after adding water)
  6. Add egg into pan and stir it
  7. Put pasta on the plate
  8. Sprinkle some black pepper
  9. Add parmesan

白醬義麵

  1. 配料(洋蔥, 洋菇, 培根)
  2. 一鍋煮水+鹽
  3. sauce pan 放奶油, 中火煎培根到微金黃
  4. 水滾後,下義麵, 就等它
  5. sauce pan 放洋蔥,煎到半透明
  6. 放洋菇,煎一下
  7. 放煮麵水
  8. 鮮奶油
  9. 黑胡椒
  10. 義麵撈起放起去 (義麵時間-1min)
  11. (optional) 放起司
