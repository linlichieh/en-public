How to use in sentences?

  • (level) China is actively pumping carbon into the environment on an enormous scale.
  • (on something) check out our video on the conflict
  • (on something) He is a world-renowned expert on food safety.
  • (comparison) The city of Paris is pratically synonymous with French.
  • (because of) The city remains a hub for food, commerce and culture, with world-renowned destinations
  • (direction) The area to the right of Seine River, better known as the Right Bank, …
  • (reach) The city’s population had grown to nearly three million people.
