Stir-Fried Water spinach (空心菜)

Preparation

  • Oil
  • Chillies x 1 (small pieces)
  • Garlic x 6 (small pieces)
  • Water spinach (3-4 pieces)
  • Salt

Process

  1. (turn on high heat) Pour oil into pan
  2. Put chillies + garlics (stir-fry it until the colour of garlics turning darker)
  3. Put water spinach into pan and stir-fry it until soft
  4. Sprinkle salt over water spinach
  5. Keep stir-fry it, but do not cook it too long

Cabbage

Preparation

  • Oil
  • Chillies
  • Garlics
  • Cabbage (cut it into medium pieces)
  • Salt

Process

  1. (turn on high heat) heat the pan
  2. When pan is very hot, add oil, garlic and chillies
  3. After stirring it for a while, put cabbage into pan
  4. Pour some water into pan and keep stirring
  5. Seasoning with salt
  6. Keep stir-fry it, but do not cook it too long
