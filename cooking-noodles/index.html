Cooking - Noodles

March 07, 2020

麻醬麵

Preparation

  • sesame sauce (amount: 3)
  • peanut butter (amount: 1)
  • soy sauce (amount: 1)
  • vinegar (amount: 1)
  • 香麻油 (amount: 1)
  • warm water (amount: 1)
  • salt
  • chili sauce
  • optional: sprint onions
  • optional: cucumbers
  • optional: spicy oil
  • optional: garlic paste

Process

noodle

  • Boil water
  • Add some salt
  • Put noodle into pot
  • Boil it util it’s ready
  • Get the noodle out of pot
  • Put some oil and cool it down

sauce

  • Put sesame sauce and peanut butter into bowl and then stir them up
  • Pour some soy sauce into bowl, and then stir it
  • Add hot water
